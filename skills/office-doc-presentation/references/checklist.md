# Office Deliverable Checklist

## Document Checklist

- Clear title and purpose
- Executive summary or opening context
- Section hierarchy that scans quickly
- Evidence or examples where claims matter
- Closing takeaway or recommendation

## Slide Checklist

- One core message per page
- No overloaded bullets
- Clear narrative from start to finish
- Visual rhythm across sections
- Final summary or call to action

## Export Checklist

- Check page breaks, line wraps, and heading consistency.
- Confirm output matches the intended audience and level of formality.
